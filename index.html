Your idea of perfect and stress-free wedding starts here

We eliminate the stress out of wedding planning so you can focus on enjoying your big day! At Lacuisine Catering, we remain conscious of your wedding budget, needs, and food preferences. We offer different menu packages that are completely adjustable.

Your wedding, your style

Whatever your style is, our team of event stylists is here to bring your vision to life! Whether it is a plated sit-down style, family-style, buffet-style, cocktail-style, and a customized set-up, we will handle every detail for you!

Plated Sit-Down Style

The most traditional reception style, a plated meal is what the majority of couples choose. This is when all the guests are seated and served a formal dinner. Typically, it consists of two courses (an appetizer and an entrée), plus dessert if it is served tableside.

Family-Style

A family-style meal is exactly as it sounds: Everyone is seated as big platters and bowls of food are passed around the table, just like you might do with your own family at home. This is a great option if you want to keep people seated at tables but do not want something as formal as a plated meal. The first course is typically served with about one or two options and the main course includes as few as a protein and two sides or multiple proteins with multiple sides.

Buffet-Style

A buffet features long tables topped with a wide variety of food options. This meal style offers the most variety for your guests, making it particularly desirable if you want a wide range of cuisines or have picky eaters. The most important thing to consider here is how to get your guests through the food lines as quickly and efficiently as possible.

Cocktail-Style

Buck tradition and do a cocktail-style wedding reception. It combats a lot of issues that couples typically face while planning their wedding, like where to sit a bunch of people who do not seem to fit at any one table. A cocktail-style reception means no seating charts, no tablescape fuss…none of that. Instead, you and your guests can mix, mingle, and dance all night long and snack on small bites (either tray-passed or laid out on a grazing table in advance) whenever you feel like it.
